About MorphDetecto

Welcome to MorphDetecto, a web application to detect if your uploaded Image is Morphed or Original. If the image is morphed, the percentage of morphing will be shown. If not, the image is safe to use. We hope you now have a clear understanding of what MorphDetecto is about.
